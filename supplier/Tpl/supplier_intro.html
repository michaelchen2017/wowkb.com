About Grand J&K Cabinetry

Grand JK Cabinetry is a quality-stock cabinet wholesaler, distributor in North America. Our products are then sold through a network of retailers, builders, contractors, designers and home centers. Grand JK has been expanding steadily since 2003.

Our cabinets come in 3″ increments. Unlike other cabinet companies in the US, we standardized our cabinets to the utmost quality, including all-wood, full overlay door, plywood construction (absolutely no particle board), heavy duty under-mounted soft-close drawer glides, dovetail drawer boxes, hidden hinges and much more. Our cabinets are semi-customize-able and easy to assemble, we also offer an extensive selection of standardized specialty cabinet and accessory items to make every kitchen unique and beautiful.
